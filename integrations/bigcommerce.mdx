title: BigCommerce
---

<Steps>
<Step title="Run the CLI">
To get started with the BigCommerce catalyst example, run the following command.

```bash
npx makeswift@latest init --template=bigcommerce-catalyst
```

</Step>

<Step title="Create a new site">
Choose your workspace, then create a new site.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/cli-pick-a-site.png" alt="Screenshot of picking a site" />
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Pick the template">
Then, choose the "BigCommerce Catalyst" example from the template picker.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/bc-catalyst-template.png" alt="Screenshot of picking a template" />
</Frame>
</Step>
</Steps>
